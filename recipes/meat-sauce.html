Meat Sauce

Description

A tomato based meat sauce that can be used with spaghetti or lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Can of tomato
  • Oregano
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Dice veggies
  2. Brown ground beef in saute pan
  3. Add vegetables to beef and cook until soft
  4. Add seasonings and cook for a few minutes
  5. Add canned tomato and cook for 30 mintes on medium low stirring occasionally
