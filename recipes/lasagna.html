Lasagna

Description

Classic italian dish made with layered noodles, cheese and spinach, and meet sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Ricotta and spinach
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Grease 9x11 casserole dish and preheat oven to 400*F
  2. Add layer of meat sauce to bottom of dish
  3. Add layer of noodles
  4. Add layer of ricotta/spinach mixture
  5. Add layer of noodles
  6. Repeat until full then top with mozarella
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes
  8. Remove foil and bake until mozzarella begins to brown
  9. Cut into equal sized squares and enjoy
