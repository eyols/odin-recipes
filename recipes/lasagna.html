Lasagna

Description

Layers of noodles cheese and saucey meat

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef and add sauce to beef
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Layer ingredients sauce, noodles, cheese, noodles and repeat until out of ingredients
  4. Bake at 350f for 1 hour
