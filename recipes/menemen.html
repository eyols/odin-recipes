Menemen

Description

Sauce of tomatoes, chiles, and onions with egg and cheese stirred in

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (either fresh diced or grated, or canned puree)
  • Onion
  • Chiles or bell peppers
  • Eggs
  • Cheese, feta or mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook chiles and onions until soft
  2. Add tomato and let simmer for about 10 minutes
  3. Stir in cheese
  4. Stir in eggs and cook until just set
