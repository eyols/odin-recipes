Cheeseburger

Description

Beef patty covered with cheese on a bun

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • American cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties slightly larger than burger buns
  2. Cook patties and top with cheese
  3. Toast bun and add toppings
  4. Place patty on bun
