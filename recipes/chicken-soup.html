Chicken Soup

Description

Soup made with chicken and vegetables. Add noodles or rice for quick and easy variations.

Ingredients

  • Chopped chicken
  • Chicken stock
  • Diced onion
  • Diced celery
  • Chopped carrots
  • Thyme
  • Sage
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook onions and celery
  2. Add seasonings and cook for one minute
  3. Add chicken broth and bring to a simmer
  4. Add chicken and carrots
  5. Simmer until carrots are cooked through and chicken is done
